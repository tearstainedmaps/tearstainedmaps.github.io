We Make Maps And Stuff

Tear Stained Maps (TSM) is a consulting firm located in Lindsay, Ontario that provides geospatial, analytical, and geomatics consulting services. Established in 2009, we have served a wide range of public and private sector clients across Canada and internationally. With the goal of aligning Geographic Information Systems (GIS) best practices with our clients’ needs, we offer customized solutions and maximize spatial data to provide the information you need to complete your project successfully.

Spatial Analysis is Hard, But We Kinda Know What We're Doing

TSM can meet your needs whether you are looking for GIS outsourcing, GIS services, or need to hire GIS mapping consultants. Our expertise and technology will help you get the location-based results that will make your organization more efficient in time and cost.
